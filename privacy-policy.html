Privacy Policy

Simple Traffic Lights for iOS  ·  Last updated: July 23, 2026

Overview

Simple Traffic Lights is a traffic light simulator for iPhone and iPad. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

Simple Traffic Lights does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require or offer any account, sign-in, or profile.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs of any kind.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your preferences (mode, colors, timer durations, layout) are kept only in the app's memory on your device while you use it. Nothing is written to a server, and nothing is synced anywhere.

In-App Purchases

Simple Traffic Lights offers optional, entirely voluntary tips through Apple's In-App Purchase system (StoreKit). These purchases are processed directly by Apple — we never see, receive, or store your payment information. Apple's handling of payment data is governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks, aside from Apple's native StoreKit framework used for the optional tips above.

Children's Privacy

Simple Traffic Lights does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
